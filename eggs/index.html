Rules:

  1. Catch the eggs
  2. White eggs increase your score by 1
  3. Gold eggs increase your score by 2
  4. Red eggs decrease your score by 1
  5. When you miss an egg (except red and black ones), your number of lives wil be reduced by 1
  6. Black eggs will decrease your score and number of lives by 1
  7. In the easy mode, the speed of the falling eggs will remain the same and you will have 6 lives
  8. In the difficult mode, the speed of the falling eggs will increase over time and you will have 5 lives
  9. The game will end when you use up all your lives
